The Tine Opener

Made from the elegant, curved tines of naturally shed Red Deer antler. Lightweight, minimal, and naturally sculptural — designed to be left out, not put away. Each piece is hand-finished and polished by a forged steel insert seated flush into the marrow.

On this object

From the tine,
the lightest hand.

The tine is the outermost branch of the antler — the slimmest, most elegant section. Its natural taper makes for an opener that feels effortless in the hand. We work the steel to follow the curve, never against it.
