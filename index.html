Mauldslie Farm, Midlothian

The farm is home to a herd of red deer. Each year, the stags naturally shed their antlers — no animal is harmed, no antler is cut. The shed antlers are collected from the hillside, cleaned, and sorted by size and character.

This is not industrial sourcing. Every antler that becomes a Stag & Steel product walked the same hills, grazed the same ground, and was shed in the same ancient cycle that red deer have followed for thousands of years.
