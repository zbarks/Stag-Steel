01 — The Material

Estate-sourced
natural material.

We work exclusively with naturally shed Red Deer antler, gathered from Mauldslie Farm in Midlothian. Each piece is a geological record of the season — marked by the weather, the diet, and the life of the stag.

02 — The Place

Highland
provenance.

Sourced from estate grounds on the edge of the Scottish lowlands. The environment is not a backdrop — it is our primary collaborator. The cold, the silence, and the rugged terrain dictate the pace of our production.

03 — The Craft

Restrained design
meets sculptural honesty.

We avoid the novelty of the rustic. Instead, we look to Scandinavian restraint and Japanese material respect. Every antler is carved to expose its inner core, then paired with hand-polished steel.
