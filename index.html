The Material

Real red deer
antler.

Antler isn't made in a factory. It's grown — by a wild animal, over a single wild season. Every length has its own colour, weight and grain. You can't fake that, and we don't try.

So no two pieces are ever the same. Yours is one of one — the only one like it in the world. That's the whole point.

How It's Made

Four steps,
by hand.

Every piece is made by hand, in small batches. No factory. No production line. No shortcuts.

01

Harvested

Antler is harvested from live red deer, then sorted by size, shape and condition.

02

Cut & matched

Each piece chosen for its part of the antler — the tine, the beam, the burr — then cut and cleaned.

03

Fitted to steel

Set onto a hand-polished stainless opener or worm, anchored to sit solid for good.

04

Sanded & finished

Sanded, washed, sealed and checked by hand before it's boxed and sent to you.
