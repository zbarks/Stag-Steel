The Idea

A Meaningful Connection

Stag & Steel creates handcrafted gifting objects made from naturally shed red deer antler collected on a family estate in Scotland. Each year the deer shed their antlers as part of a natural cycle. These antlers are gathered and transformed into functional objects designed to last a lifetime. No two pieces are the same. The material itself carries the landscape it came from.

The Material

Naturally Shed, Never Manufactured

Every Stag & Steel piece begins with a naturally shed antler. Red deer shed their antlers each year as part of a biological cycle. The antlers fall naturally from the animal and are collected from the hillside. No animals are harmed and no antlers are cut. It is an estate-sourced natural material, offering quiet luxury and absolute provenance.

The Place

Mauldslie Farm, Scotland

Mauldslie Farm sits in the Moorfoot Hills of Midlothian, Scotland overlooking Gladhouse Reservoir. The surrounding landscape is home to a herd of native red deer whose antlers become the raw material for Stag & Steel objects. Each piece is a small part of this landscape made tangible.

The Craft

Finished By Hand

We embrace Scandinavian restraint and Japanese respect for materials. Handcrafted production means each piece is individually finished. We do not manufacture tools; we create sculptural, timeless, collectible objects that connect you to the wild.
